Pollution

There are differnent types of pollution such as air pollution, water pollution, noise pollution and soil pollution.

Air Pollution

Air pollution is when chemicals are being realsed into the air throug harmful gases, burning of fuels, and other byproducts of modern human life.

Land Pollution

Waste materials produced by humans are left to accumulate on the earths surface. Many chemicals, such as pesticides and fertilizer,are absorbed into soil.

Noise Pollution

Humans produce excessive amounts of loud noise,including industrial and vehicle sources.

Water Pollution

Toxins from humans and industrial waste are introduced into our water supplies.

Light Pollution

Humans produce excessive amounts of artifical light that interferes with natural cycles of light and darkness in the environment.
